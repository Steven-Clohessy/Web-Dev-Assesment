Earth - Our Home Planet

The third planet from the Sun, Earth is the only known planet with life. Our beautiful blue marble is home to millions of species and provides everything we need to survive and thrive.

The Crust - Our Foundation

Earth's crust is like the skin of an apple - very thin compared to the whole planet. It's made of solid rock and is where all life exists. The oceanic crust is thinner (3-6 miles) while continental crust is thicker (up to 30 miles).

The Mantle - Earth's Engine

The mantle makes up 84% of Earth's volume! It's mostly solid rock that flows very slowly due to extreme heat and pressure. This movement drives plate tectonics, creating earthquakes, volcanoes, and mountain ranges.

The Outer Core - Our Magnetic Shield

This layer of liquid iron and nickel is responsible for Earth's magnetic field. As it moves and flows, it creates electrical currents that generate our protective magnetic field, shielding us from harmful solar radiation.
